About us

Yes, you got it! The restaurant’s name is derived from the major intersection of our first restaurant, which is situated at Markham and Progress. We take a deep pride in being a family restaurant in Canada and in serving the Canadian multicultural society! We serve authentic Indian and Italian foods. Pay us a visit once and you won’t regret it! You can dine in or take out and we offer free Delivery on orders above $50.00! Birthday Cake will be provided free - compliment of The Progress Restaurant!
